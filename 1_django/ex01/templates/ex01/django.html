Introduction to Django

Django is a high-level Python web framework that encourages rapid development and clean, pragmatic design. Developed by a group of developers at the Lawrence Journal-World newspaper in 2003, Django was initially created to meet the fast-paced requirements of a newsroom.

Since its official release in 2005, Django has grown into a popular framework that emphasizes reusable code, a comprehensive built-in admin interface, and a focus on the DRY (Don't Repeat Yourself) principle, making it suitable for both small and large projects.
